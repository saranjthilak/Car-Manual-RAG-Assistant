MG ZS Warning Messages Manual

Engine Oil Pressure Warning

This warning light indicates that the engine oil pressure is too low. Stop the vehicle safely and switch off the engine immediately. Check the engine oil level and contact an MG service center if the issue persists.

Brake System Warning

This warning light indicates a problem with the braking system or low brake fluid level. Stop driving as soon as it is safe and inspect the brake fluid. Contact a qualified technician immediately.

Battery Charging System Warning

This warning indicates that the battery is not charging correctly. Electrical systems may stop functioning. Visit an authorized MG workshop as soon as possible.

Tyre Pressure Monitoring System Warning

One or more tyres have low pressure. Reduce speed, avoid harsh braking, and inflate tyres to the recommended pressure.

Gasoline Particulate Filter Full Warning

The gasoline particulate filter is becoming full due to soot accumulation. Continue driving at a steady speed to allow automatic regeneration of the filter. Avoid switching off the engine during regeneration. If the warning remains on, contact an MG service center.

Coolant Temperature Warning

The engine coolant temperature is too high. Stop the vehicle safely and allow the engine to cool down. Check coolant levels before continuing.
